Why k=5 Over k=8?

  • Elbow Method: Clear inflection at k=5 (15.5% → 12.3% diminishing returns)
  • Silhouette: k=5 is 0.353 vs k=8 peak 0.364 (+3% marginal gain)
  • Business Context: 5 distinct strategies vs 8 = execution complexity
  • Cost-Benefit: 3% statistical gain doesn't justify 60% more operational burden
