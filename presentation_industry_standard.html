Slide 3: Elbow Method - Complete Analysis

What is the Elbow Method?

The Elbow Method analyzes Inertia (Within-Cluster Sum of Squares - how tightly points cluster together). It identifies the optimal k by finding where inertia reduction plateaus, forming an "elbow" shape in the curve.

Slide 4: Silhouette Score Analysis

What is Silhouette Score?

Measures cluster quality by evaluating how similar points are within their cluster versus neighboring clusters. Range: -1 to 1 (higher = better separation). Formula: (b(i) - a(i)) / max(a(i), b(i))
