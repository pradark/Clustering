Why k=5 Over k=8?

  • Elbow Method: Clear inflection at k=5 (15.5% improvement → 12.3% diminishing returns)
  • Silhouette Score: k=5 at 0.353 is "good"; k=8 peak at 0.364 is only +3% marginal gain
  • Business Feasibility: 5 distinct strategies are operationally manageable; 8 would be overwhelming
  • Cost-Benefit: 3% statistical improvement doesn't justify 60% additional operational complexity
Limitations: Snapshot data (not time-series), 6-12 month tenure range, behavioral/financial metrics only (no demographics).
Next Steps: Historical backtesting, segment migration tracking, time-series prediction models, demographic integration.
